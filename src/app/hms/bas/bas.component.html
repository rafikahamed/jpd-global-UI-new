All foreign sellers are required to collect GST when selling and importing goods to Australia. Some of the key points for foreign sellers include:

. Registering for GST reference number (ARN)

. Providing this ARN number to exporter/importer of the goods

. Paying GST to ATO

. Preparing and lodging Business Activity Statement (BAS)

. Claiming back the GST from re-exported goods
